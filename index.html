SHOP
Nearby electronics shops supply essential components like resistors, capacitors, transistors, and microcontrollers, as well as tools like multimeters and soldering kits. They enable students to quickly gather materials for hands-on projects, and some even offer custom PCB services, supporting efficient project building and troubleshooting.






PROJECTS
Previous electronics projects include smart energy meters, automatic street lighting, solar-powered chargers, DC motor control, and temperature monitoring systems. These projects involve practical applications like power management, renewable energy, and automation, providing students with hands-on experience in circuit design and system implementation.
